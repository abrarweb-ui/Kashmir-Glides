Our Story

Born from the Valley's Soul

Kashmir Glide Tour & Travels was born from a deep love for this land. Based in Iqbal Colony, Tangmarg — the very gateway to Gulmarg — we have been crafting authentic Kashmir experiences for travellers from across India and the world.

Our founders grew up watching the seasons transform Kashmir — cherry blossoms in spring, shikara rides in summer, golden chinar in autumn, pristine snow in winter. That intimate connection is what makes every tour we design truly special.

Our philosophy: every traveller deserves a journey that feels personal, not packaged.

Founded in Tangmarg, the heart of Kashmir's tourism belt
500+ happy travellers from 20+ Indian states and abroad
Fluent guides in Hindi, English, Kashmiri and Urdu
